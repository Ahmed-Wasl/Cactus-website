CARE AND HEALTH FOR
YOUR CACRTUS

  • In cold times, add water once a month and
    during the summer do it when the soil is
    very dry.
  • Have good drainage so that the cactus does
    not accumulate water.
  • Place your cactus or succulent in a location
    with indirect light.
  • Do not water more than necessary, as it can
    accumulate too much water and the plant
    will rot.
  • Do not expose to high temperatures, as
    succulent plants are not fans of extremes.
